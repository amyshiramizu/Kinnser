üìã Kinnser Med Filler

How to Use:

  1. Parse medications in the "Parse Meds" tab
  2. Click "Copy Meds for Kinnser"
  3. Go to Kinnser ‚Üí Patient ‚Üí Add New Medication
  4. Click the "Kinnser Med Filler" bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar
  5. First time: it will ask you to paste the data - just Cmd+V and OK
  6. It fills in the form fields automatically
  7. Verify the data, select Yes/No for coverage, click Save (or Save and Add Additional)
  8. Click the bookmarklet again for the next medication
  9. Repeat until all meds are entered!
